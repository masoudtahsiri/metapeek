MetaPeek Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 19, 2024

Introduction

MetaPeek is a Chrome extension developed by Refact.co that helps users analyze and optimize their webpage's meta tags and SEO. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our extension.

Data Collection and Processing

MetaPeek operates entirely locally in your browser. We do not collect or store any personal data. The extension only:

  • Reads the current webpage's meta tags and SEO elements
  • Analyzes this information to provide SEO recommendations
  • Shows the analysis results in the extension popup

Data Storage

All data processing happens in your browser:

  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No data is stored permanently
  • All analysis data is cleared when you close the extension popup
  • The only storage used is Chrome's temporary memory during the current session

Permissions We Use

MetaPeek requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To read the current webpage's content
  • scripting: To analyze webpage elements
  • storage: To temporarily store analysis results during your session

Your Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. MetaPeek:

  • Does not track your browsing history
  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not share any data with third parties
  • Does not use any analytics or tracking tools
